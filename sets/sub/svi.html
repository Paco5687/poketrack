Scarlet & Violet Base

Begin a New Adventure with Pokémon ex!

Set out for a journey in the Paldea region! Meet first partners
Sprigatito, Fuecoco, and Quaxly, and explore the power of the Legendary
Pokémon Koraidon and Miraidon as Pokémon ex. Other Pokémon ex evolve
their way into battle—including Arcanine and Gyarados with a dazzling
new look. Even more Pokémon appear as illustration rare cards with amazing
artwork—discover them all in the Pokémon TCG: Scarlet & Violet expansion!
